# Surveys

## Introduction
A survey was conducted to collect information from potential users of the FashionPanda platform. The survey aimed to understand customer preferences, shopping habits, and expectations from an online fashion delivery service.

## Survey Objectives
- Understand customer shopping behavior
- Identify challenges in traditional and online fashion shopping
- Determine the demand for a fashion delivery platform
- Gather feature requirements from potential users

## Survey Details
- Number of Participants: 50
- Target Audience: Students, working professionals, and online shoppers
- Method: Online questionnaire

## Survey Questions
1. How often do you buy fashion products?
2. Do you prefer online shopping or physical stores?
3. What problems do you face while shopping?
4. Would you use a platform that connects multiple local clothing stores?
5. Which features are most important to you?

## Survey Results
- 70% prefer online shopping.
- 80% want fast delivery services.
- 65% find it difficult to compare products across different stores.
- 75% are interested in a single platform for local fashion retailers.
- The most requested features were:
  - Real-time order tracking
  - Secure payment options
  - Easy returns and refunds
  - Product search and filtering

## Conclusion
The survey results indicate strong demand for a centralized fashion delivery platform like FashionPanda that offers convenience, variety, and efficient delivery services.
