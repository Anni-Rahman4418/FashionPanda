# Information Gathering

## Introduction
To understand user needs and system requirements for FashionPanda, information was gathered using multiple methods including surveys, interviews, and market research.

## Methods Used

### 1. Surveys
Online surveys were designed to collect data from potential customers regarding their shopping habits, preferences, and challenges in fashion shopping.

### 2. Interviews
Interviews were conducted with both customers and local clothing retailers to understand their expectations and difficulties in online and offline fashion sales.

### 3. Market Research
Existing fashion e-commerce platforms were analyzed to identify features, limitations, and opportunities for improvement.

## Key Findings
- Customers prefer fast and convenient shopping experiences
- Local retailers lack digital presence
- Users want real-time product availability
- Delivery speed is a major decision factor

## Conclusion
The gathered information confirms the need for a centralized fashion delivery platform like FashionPanda.
